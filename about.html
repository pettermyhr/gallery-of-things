About

Documentary and fine art photographer based in Oslo, Norway. Capturing quiet moments and the poetry of everyday life through analog and digital mediums.

Editorial photography, portrait sessions, documentary projects, commercial campaigns, and fine art prints. Available for both studio and on-location shoots worldwide.

Rates vary depending on project scope, usage rights, and deliverables. Please reach out with your project details for a custom quote. Day rates start at €800.

Collaborated with The New York Times, Vogue Scandinavia, National Geographic, Moma Oslo, and various independent artists and cultural institutions across Europe.

World Press Photo nominee 2023, featured in British Journal of Photography, and exhibited at Nordic Light International Festival of Photography.
